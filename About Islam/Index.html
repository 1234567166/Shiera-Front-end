Islam In Quran

Islam in the Qur’an is described as the true path of submission to Allah (God)
and the continuation of the guidance given to earlier prophets like Ibrahim (Abraham), Musa (Moses),
and ‘Isa (Jesus, peace be upon them). The word “Islam” itself means submission
or surrender to the will of Allah.

Prophet Muhammed

Prophet Muhammad (peace and blessings be upon him) is the final prophet in Islam and the messenger through
whom Allah revealed the Qur’an. Muslims believe that he was sent as “Rahmatan lil-‘Alameen” (a mercy to all worlds)
to guide humanity to the worship of one God.
